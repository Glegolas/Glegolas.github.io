Time Period: 15XX Location: Tacoma, Washington ========================= Character 1 and Character 2 have been best friends since childhood. They both met each other in an orphanage where the two bonded over shared experiences of getting expelled from school through antics derived from sheer boredom, and their disatisfaction with common life that makes them feel like outcasts in their society. Both despise the unfair treatment of 'peasants' and the hypocricy of the upperclasses. Character 2 is seen as highly intellgient and regarded as a prodigy in architectural engineering. Character 2 has had a vast thirst for knowledge ever since the age of three and has spent most of their life with their head inside a book or in a new experiment. Character 2 is fond of Character 1 for their conviction in freedom and their want to help those in need. Character 1 is your average kid, but has extremely gifted strength that has been able to aid Character 2 in building their elaborate designs. Character 1 is fond of Character 2 for their high intellect and want to create a better future. Character 2 has a dream of building a highly advanced city where anybody can achieve their dreams. Character 1 convinces Character 2 that they should get started. At the ages of 14, both character's escape the orphanage and begin work on their new utopia in the outskirts. ========================= Act 1:
